# KeenKeeper

Your personal shelf of meaningful connections. Keep track of the friends who matter most in your life — and never let an important relationship fade away without noticing.

---

## What is this?

We all have friends we care about, but life gets busy. You forget to check in. Weeks turn into months. KeenKeeper is a simple app that helps you stay on top of your friendships — it shows you who you've been in touch with recently, who you're starting to drift from, and who really needs a catch-up soon.

Think of it like a to-do list, but for the people you love.

---

## Technologies Used

- **Next.js** — for building the UI and server-side rendering
- **Next.js App Router** — for page navigation and routing
- **Tailwind CSS** — for styling
- **Recharts** — for the stats and data visualization
- **React Toastify** — for notifications
- **Slipper** — for loading data
- **Vercel** — for deployment

---

## Key Features

**1. Friend Tracking Dashboard**
See all your friends in one place with their last interaction date and a status — whether they're "on track", "almost due", or "overdue" for a catch-up. No more forgetting who you haven't talked to in a while.

**2. Relationship Stats**
Get a quick overview of how your social life is doing — total friends, how many are on track, how many need attention, and your total interactions this month. It's surprisingly motivating to see the numbers.

**3. Timeline View**
A timeline of your past interactions so you can look back and remember when you last connected with someone and what you did together.
